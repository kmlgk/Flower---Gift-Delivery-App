Our story

Started with one bouquet, one bicycle, one promise.

In 2019 we set out to fix a simple problem: gifts arriving late, wilted, or without a way to know when they'd show up. Today Bloomora connects local florists, bakers and gift stylists with a live-tracked delivery fleet across 90+ cities — so every "I was thinking of you" lands exactly when it should.
